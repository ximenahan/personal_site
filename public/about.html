About

Posted on Sat, Aug 24, 2024

薇根斯坦 Vittgenstein

I have crafted distinct identities, each embodying a unique facet and individual pursuit of self. To nurture and fortify these facets, I assign them names and visual representations.

The five of them can be categorized into two categories: ‘Soulful Yearnings’ and ‘Love & Connections’.

The first category comprises three identities: Zenobja Jez, Esperanza Orue, and Akasha O’Clery. Each of these identities symbolizes a distinct realm, namely politics, humanities, and spirituality, respectively.

Within the second category, there are two identities: Herbert Franklin, Jessica Chen, Ximena Elizeche. These identities symbolize my yearning for fulfilling an ordinary life, deep affection for my children, and meaningful connections with friends, family, and partners, respectively.

Each identity has been assigned an account and will contribute content pertaining to their respective domains. My aspiration is for all of them to thrive in harmony and grow well-balanced.

Herbert Franklin

My desire is to live as an ordinary individual, just like everyone else in this world, leading a life that is both simple and modest. I want to enjoy the plain, everyday happiness and gradually grow old with contentment.

Esperanza Orue

My endless love for the humanities and social sciences drives me in two ways.

The former compels me to reclaim the boundless vitality and life force of the spiritual world for Earth's society, experiencing the grandeur and vastness of the human spirit.

The latter pushes me to understand and seek solutions for the most critical social, political, and economic problems, ultimately striving to build a sustainable society for all of us.

Zenobja Jez

In this unbearable, ugly, and cruel world, where everyone's living space has been significantly compressed, politics will always remain the core of world's operations, regardless of how the world changes.

I aspire to establish a nonprofit organization with real global power, possessing comprehensive strength and networks to solve the most difficult problems in the harshest places on earth. First, we must gain political influence in America, and then extend and apply this influence on a global scale.

Akasha O'Clery

I have two ultimate pursuits.

The first is my quest for spirituality, where I strive to cultivate a lifestyle of peacefulness, satisfaction, and stability while transcending personal desires and primal urges as much as possible.

The second is uncovering the truth of the world, seeking to understand the deeper meanings of life, humanity, personal journeys, and the vast, boundless universe.

Ximena Elizeche

When I am about to die and look back on my entire life, I hope the most valuable things will be the precious relationships I have built. The connections with children, partners, family, and friends will tell a story of love, trust, interdependence, mutual understanding, shared memories, a sense of stability, and endless happiness.
